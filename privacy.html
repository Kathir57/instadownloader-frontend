Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025. We believe privacy is a right, not a privilege.

1. Data Collection

InstaGet does not collect, store, or sell any personally identifiable information. The Instagram URL you paste is used only to complete your request and discarded immediately. We do not log your IP address, name, email, or account info.

2. Media Files

We act as an intermediary to retrieve publicly available media from Instagram's servers. We do not store downloaded media files, cache any content, or access private accounts. All media streams directly from Instagram's CDN to your device.

3. Cookies

InstaGet does not use tracking, advertising, or analytics cookies. Minimal session cookies strictly necessary for operation may be used. These contain no personal data and expire when you close your browser.

4. Third-Party Services

Our site loads fonts from Google Fonts (fonts.googleapis.com). Google may collect usage data per their privacy policy. We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any third-party tracking scripts.

5. Children's Privacy

InstaGet is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If you believe a child has used our service, contact us immediately.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted here with an updated date. Continued use of InstaGet constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

7. Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy? We're committed to responding promptly.
